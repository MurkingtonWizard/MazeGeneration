SquarePush
Controls: 
    To move Ninja-se, either press the arrow buttons or 
        click once on the game board and use WASD. 
    To remove a 2x2 group, left-click on the 2x2 group.
Buttons:
    Level 1: Starts the 5x5 game.
    Level 2: Starts the 4x4 game.
    Level 3: Starts the 6x6 game.
    Reset: Restarts the current game.
Win condition:
    You win once all colors (except Ninja-se) are removed
        from the game board.
